#Condition 3: Homoscedasticity

bartlett_stats= bartlett(predictions, errors)
levene_stats= levene(predictions, errors)

print('The Levene test shows a T-stat of {:3g} and a P-value of {}'.format(levene_stats[0], levene_stats[1]))

The Levene test shows a T-stat of 2321.24 and a P-value of 0.0

Test rejects null hypothesis-results are heteroscedastic. Model may overpredict
